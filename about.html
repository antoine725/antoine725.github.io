Hi! My name is Antoine Bari, but many people know me as Grizzou. I'm a 20-year-old engineering student from Grenoble, France, with a wide range of interests. I love building things, whether it's through electronics, coding, or creative projects.

Beyond my studies, I'm really into music. I've been playing guitar since I was about 10, after my dad showed me how to (poorly) play the only song he knew. I also took two to three years of piano lessons, as well as bass guitar lessons. I'd consider myself a music enthusiast, with a good ear for music and a constant curiosity for discovering new albums and genres. I'm working on releasing rap projects with some friends.

Creativity — whether through writing, projects, or simply capturing moments — is something I try to keep at the center of what I do.

I also enjoy sports, especially hiking and climbing. I love the outdoors and the challenge of reaching new heights and summits (the more summits, the better!). I try every day to push my limits and try new things. Right now, I'm practicing calisthenics and exploring trail running in the mountains around Grenoble to challenge myself in new ways.

This website is a collection of my interests, projects, and experiences. I hope you find something here that resonates with you!
